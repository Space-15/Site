Development Blog

21 Mar 2019: Added saving and loading ability to inventory to have persistant components and items accross sessions.

19 Mar 2019: Implemented components into the map which need to be collected by the player to progress to different planets and ultimately complete the game.

18 Mar 2019: Added a quit button to the main menu so that the application can be exited at any time.

11 Mar 2019: Improved visibility in caves due to some devices having low brightness making some scenarios difficult to view.

05 Mar 2019: Added lightning effect on specific maps. Walking into the lightning will mean that the player will lose health.

05 Mar 2019: Added cutscenes for the story mode. Cutscenes introduce the story and are triggered throughout the playthrough of the game as objectives are achieved.

02 Mar 2019: Added snow effect on specific maps. This effect is in the form of particles that fall accross the screen and move relative to the player camera to add depth to the world.

28 Feb 2019: Started using the GUT Framework for regular Unit Testing which will help us to add new features seamlessly by allowing us to test functionality of old features quickly.

28 Feb 2019: Improved main menu and added parallax effect so that menus are no longer static and have a consistent style amongst them.

27 Feb 2019: Added animations for water tiles. These tiles will be placed in places where there is water on the map and will be predominantly on the water planet.

22 Feb 2019: Added fog and particle effects which move relative to the player to give a feeling of depth.

20 Feb 2019: Implemented first versions of new maps. These will be the 3 planets that the player will have to make his way through to complete the game.

16 Feb 2019: Added torch ability for player to be used when navigating caves and dark areas.

14 Feb 2019: Changed fixed resolution to 1080p for fullscreen gameplay.

13 Feb 2019: Implemented dynamic save and load functions to allow players to save game progress and be able to load at a later date.

07 Feb 2019: Updated UI, oxygen and health bars are now in top left instead of above player.

29 Jan 2019: Updated tutorial mode to have dialogue boxes.

28 Nov 2018: Oxygen mechanic fully implemented in the game. Now oxygen can be affected by the environment aswell as replenished by oxygen tanks.

23 Nov 2018: Music and sound effects have been partially implemented.

10 Nov 2018: Health and oxygen bar animations connected to player properties. Health and damage is now fully functional, oxygen still to be implemented.

06 Nov 2018: A tutorial mode has been added to the game, allowing the player to get familiar with the game before they start playing.

3 Planets to Explore

You will first land on planet Zuice, a thunder and lighting planet, from there you will then move onto Picydon, the water planet, where you must manouver around over the seas. Finally you will land on Heydies, the infamous underworld planet, themed with obstacles such as lava and fire.

Collectables

When exploring each planet, you will discover different parts and pieces which you will need in order to get your spaceship together to leave for planet earth. In addition to these you will also find consumables which will increase your health and oxygen levels.
